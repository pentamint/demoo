Incorporating a highly exclusive aesthetic concepts

 

- architectural tailoring and flowing basic silhouette, pure beauty and

 

maturity, black and white, maximalism and minimalism -

 

Demoo is characterized by the beauty of its bold contrast

 

while presenting gentle, harmonic vibe.

 

Choonmoo Park launched a designer brand, Demoo, in 1988

 

which marked the beginning of the ‘modern avant-garde look’ in Korea

 

with her unique iteration of contemporary fashion and philosophy.

 

Demoo continues to evolve today by embracing both artistic and

 

commercial values all the more successfully.
